Services

Services

We are the first farming corporation in Nigeria to receive an award on Hygiene Meat Supply. We deliver crops, egss and dressed meat of poultry and other farm animals to top stores in Abuja and environs. Our drive is to make food affordable for the teeming masses in need of affordable and edible food.

We are your best option if you are new in farming. We offer you top tier service in crop farming setup, animal farm setup and input supplies. We have a competent team of field workers that are poised to ensuring you have the maximum output for every farm investment you venture into. See below range of services we offer.
